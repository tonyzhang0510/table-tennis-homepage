Equipment

A paddle is a wooden blade with a sheet of rubber glued to each side. Each part shapes how the ball behaves — speed, spin, and control.

Blades

Defensive

Slow and flexible. Built for absorbing pace.

All-Round

Balanced speed and control. A common starting point.

Offensive

Fast and stiff. Made for aggressive looping.

Offensive+

The fastest category. Demands precise technique.

Rubbers

Control

Soft and forgiving. Easy to handle.

Spin

Tacky surface. Generates heavy spin.

Tensor

Pre-tensioned sponge for explosive speed.

Anti-spin

Neutralizes incoming spin. A defensive tool.

Long Pips

Long pimples that reverse spin and disrupt rallies.
