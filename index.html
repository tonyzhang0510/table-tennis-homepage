The Basics

Scoring

Games are played to 11 points, win by 2. Matches are best of 5 or best of 7.

Serving

Players alternate serves every two points. The ball is tossed from an open palm.

The Rally

The ball must bounce once on each side. A player loses the point if they can't return it.
